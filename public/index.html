Data Privacy Notice

Your Privacy Matters

In compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines, please read this notice carefully before participating.

🏫
Who Is Collecting Your Data

This survey is conducted by undergraduate students of the Department of Information Technology, USTP Claveria Campus as part of IT 324 – Quantitative Methods (S.Y. 2025–2026), under the supervision of the course instructor.

Research Team: Vanessa Caroline Abrazado · Tristan Amiel Clemen · Rex Dominic Sihay · Princess Grace Marie Sitoy
Contact: ezqueue.system@gmail.com

📋
What Data We Collect

This survey collects the following non-personally identifiable information only:

  • Your general role at USTP Claveria (e.g., Student, Faculty, Staff)
  • Which student service offices you visit (e.g., Registrar, Cashier)
  • How frequently you visit those offices per semester
  • Your Likert-scale ratings on queueing experience (numerical scores only)
  • Optional open-ended text responses about your experience
  • The date and time your response was submitted (for record-keeping only)
⚠ We do not collect your name, student/employee ID, email address, contact number, IP address, device information, or any data that can directly identify you as an individual.
🎯
Why We Collect It (Purpose)

Your data will be used exclusively for the following academic purposes:

  • To assess the current queueing experience at USTP Claveria student service offices
  • To determine the need for a digital queue management system
  • To support the research paper submitted for IT 324 – Quantitative Methods
  • To generate aggregate statistical summaries (averages, frequencies) for the research findings

Your responses will never be used for commercial purposes, sold to third parties, or shared outside the research team and supervising instructor.

🔒
Who Has Access to Your Data

Access to collected data is strictly limited to:

  • The research team members listed above
  • The IT 324 course instructor / research adviser
  • USTP Claveria academic administrators, if required for research evaluation

Data is stored in a secured cloud database accessible only through credentials held exclusively by the research team. No third-party analytics or advertising tools are connected to this system.

🗓️
Data Retention and Disposal
  • All raw response records will be retained only until the final submission and evaluation of the research paper — no later than end of S.Y. 2025–2026
  • After this period, all individual response records will be permanently and irreversibly deleted from the database
  • Aggregate statistical summaries (e.g., mean scores, frequency tables) may be retained as part of the published research record without identifying any individual
⚖️
Your Rights Under RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012)

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Be informed — know how your data is collected, used, and stored (this notice)
  • Withdraw — stop participating at any point before clicking Submit; no partial data will be saved
  • Access — request a copy of any data attributed to your session
  • Erasure / Blocking — request deletion of your response by contacting us with your submission date and time
  • Lodge a complaint — contact the National Privacy Commission (NPC) at privacy.gov.ph if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, email the research team at ezqueue.system@gmail.com.

🛡️
Anonymity Guarantee

This survey is fully anonymous. The demographic information collected in Part I (role, offices, frequency) is used only for aggregate grouping in statistical analysis and cannot be used to identify any individual respondent. The research team has no technical means to link a response to a specific person.
