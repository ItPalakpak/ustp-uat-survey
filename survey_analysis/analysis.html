Statistical instruments used
Frequency & Percentage
% = (f ÷ N) × 100
Used to describe respondent profile distributions and categorical response summaries. f = frequency of a specific response; N = total respondents.
Weighted Mean (WM)
WM = Σ(f · x) ÷ N
Applied per Likert item to compute an average agreement score. x = rating value (1–5); f = frequency at each value.
Standard Deviation (SD)
SD = √[Σ(xᵢ − x̄)² ÷ (n−1)]
Measures consistency of respondent ratings. A low SD signals broad agreement; a high SD signals divergent experiences.
